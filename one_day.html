Rohtang Pass

Altitude:

13,054 Ft

Best Season: March to November
Rohtang pass is famous for different reasons, one of being presence of snow throughout the year which attracts tourist and adventure lovers. Another reason being the spectacular view it offers. This pass lies at the elevation of 13,054 ft and happens to be the gate way to arid Lahul and Spiti valley. It also connects to the Ladakh region.

This pass provides beautiful views of glaciers and Chandra River. A famous twin peak called Geypen is also visible from here. Rohtang pass acts like a rain shadow area for Lahul and Spiti valley.

Altitude:

Lamadug Trek

Altitude:

9,900 Ft

Best Season: March to December
This trek starts from Manali itself. The Lamadugh Trek is an ideal trek to introduce oneself to undeniable allure of adventure. This trek gives you the peace from the crowd of Manali.

Gaining the height of 3200ft this trek takes you through the kaleidoscope of panoramic views the nature offers. It begins from Hadimba temple and takes you through a trail of oak, cedar and maple trees. The trek is uphill climb up to the Lamadugh .
