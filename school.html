Camp Junga, Shimla ( H.P.)

Nestled in the dense pine forests in the hills of Himachal Pradesh Junga is 26 km away from the city of Shimla and 18 km from Chail. Calm and serene campsite yet well connected to the other major cities of Himachal Pradesh.

Camp Manali, Manali (H.P.)

The camp is situated on the left bank of river Beas. Two mountain ranges Dhauladhar and Pirpanjal surround the camp and add beauty to it. The main highlight of the camp is that it lies in an apple orchard and close to hot Sulphur springs.

Camp Tirthan, Tirthan valley (H.P.)

With the backdrop of steep tall mountains, Tirthan camp has been a favorite adventure spot for every age group. The icy cold water of thunderous Tirthan river offers real wilderness challenges and adventure for trekkers, adventurers and bird watchers .

Camp Tamia, Madhya Pradesh

One of the hidden treasures of Madhya Pradesh, Tamia is a picturesque forest destination that offers scenic and breath-taking views of dense forests and mountains. Untouched and unexplored, Tamia is the perfect location to disconnect from the world and experience absolute tranquility.

Camp Sitlakhet, Uttrakhand

Pinewood Cliff, Sitlakhet is located in Uttarakhand 30 km ahead of Ranikhet. Nestled in Kumaon Himalayas, at a height of 7500ft, it is a quiet and picturesque campsite offering a clear 180Â° view of the Himalayan Peaks, with Nanda Devi standing tall. The camp is located approximately 375 km from Delhi and is in close proximity to excellent tourist destinations such as Nainital.
